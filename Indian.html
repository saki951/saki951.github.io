Indian Cuisine

The Indian Cuisine encompasses the diverse regional and traditional dishes that are native to India. The cuisines differ from region to region with each region having different spices, herbs, and vegetables. Indian cuisine has it role in other differnt cusines. India has a historical part in the spice trade that occured around the 15th century.

RECIPES

Chicken Biryani

Who doesn't love biryani? Biryani is one of the most popular dish across the Indian subcontinent and is also very popular among its diaspora. Biryani is a rice dish made with Indian spices and herbs. It can be made with chicken, beef, fish, lamb, egg, and/or vegetables. There's many different variations of biryani, but for this specific one, we'll be making chicken biryani. The recipe can be found here.

Butter Chicken

If you never had butter chicken, this is your sign to have it today. Butter chicken is a type of chicken curry that is made with tomato gravy, butter, and cream. Fun fact: butter chicken was unintentionally created. It was made by chance in the 1950s, but this (great) accident has made one of the best dishes in the Indian cuisine. The recipe can be found here.

Chicken 65

Not sure if you just want a snack or a full meal? No worries, this dish server as both. Chicken 65 is a dish where the chicken is marinated and then deep-fried. One of the key ingredients used is red chile peppers. There are many different variation of Chicken 65 and it deserves all the hype. The recipe can be found here.
