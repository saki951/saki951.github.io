THE FOOD DIARY

Italian Cuisine

Italian cusisne is a subdivision of the Mediterranean cuisine. It consists of recipes and ingredients that have flourished throughout the Italic Peninsula. The cusine varies across the different regions of the peninsula. Italian cuisine is one of the most popular and widespread cusines in the world and has influenced many other cusines. It especially has roots in the United States.

Carbonara Pasta

If you're not sure what to cook, pasta is always the best bet. Carbonara is a pasta dish that originated in Rome and may have been made as early as the 1830s. It consists of hard cheese, egg, and some type of meat. The recipe for Carbonara is found here.

Margherita Pizza

I think we can all agree that pizza is one of the best food ever created. Margherita pizza is a common Naples-style pizza and part of the Neapolitan cuisine. Margherita pizza is made with San Marzano tomatoes(a type of plum tomato), mozzarella cheese, basil, and olive oil. The recipe for this popular dish can be found here.

Minestrone Soup

Minestrone is a type of Italian soup. It's a thick soup made with vegetables and pasta or rice. The usual ingredients most people use are celery, tomatos, beans, carrots, croutons, and onions. It also usually has a meat-based or vegetable-based broth. One of the great aspects of this dish is that there's no actual set recipe. Most people make this soup with whatever ingredients are at their disposal. A recipe to one of its varieties can be found here.
