THE FOOD DIARY

Japansese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine focuses on the regional and traditional food and ingredients of Japan. Japanese cuisine has an emphasis on seasonal ingredients. Many of their dishes consist of seafood, pickeled vegetables, and ingredients cooked in broth. They also have many deep-fried dishes such as tempura. Japanese cuisine is full of rich ingredients and a strong history.

Omurice

Omurice is a very popular dish in Japan that originated in teh 20th century. It is an example of two different cuisines being mixed, Japanes and Western. Omurice consists of an omelette made with fried rice and scrambled eggs and is commonly topped with ketchup. The recipe for the delicious dish can be found here.

Shoyu Ramen

Shoyu ramen is a popular dish that can easily be made in the comfort of your home. The word 'Shoyu' means soy sauce in Japanese, so as you probably have guessed, Shoyu Ramen is ramen made with soy sauce-based broth. It's a quick, easy dish that can have a variety of toppings such as egg, meat, tempura, and vegetables making it the perfect, flavorful dish. The recipe can be found here.

Okonomiyaki

This is an item that can be served as breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Okonomiyaki is a Japansese pancake containing various ingredients such as cabbage and eggs, all mixed together with a flour-based batter. There's countless combinations of ingredients and toppings that can be used for Okonomiyaki. It's a very popular street food, served in many regions across Japan.
